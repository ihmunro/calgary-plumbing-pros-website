Water Heater Maintenance Guide

May 28, 2023

Regular maintenance of your water heater is crucial for optimal performance and longevity. Here's your comprehensive guide to water heater maintenance.

Annual Maintenance Checklist

  • Check the pressure relief valve
  • Flush the tank to remove sediment
  • Inspect the anode rod
  • Check for leaks
  • Test the temperature settings

Signs Your Water Heater Needs Attention

  • Rusty or discolored water
  • Strange noises
  • Inconsistent water temperature
  • Visible leaks
  • Reduced hot water capacity
