How to Prevent Frozen Pipes This Winter

June 1, 2023

Calgary's winters can be harsh on your plumbing system. Here's your comprehensive guide to preventing frozen pipes and protecting your home.

Why Pipes Freeze

When temperatures drop below freezing, the water inside your pipes can freeze and expand, potentially causing pipes to burst. This typically occurs in:

  • Pipes exposed to severe cold
  • Plumbing in unheated interior spaces
  • Pipes against exterior walls

Prevention Tips

Follow these essential steps to protect your pipes:

  1. Insulate pipes in unheated areas
  2. Keep garage doors closed
  3. Open kitchen and bathroom cabinet doors
  4. Let cold water drip from faucets
  5. Maintain consistent heating throughout your home

Emergency Steps

If you suspect frozen pipes:

  • Keep faucets open
  • Apply heat safely
  • Call professional help if needed
